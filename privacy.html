Privacy Policy

Patator

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Patator (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes, why it is used, and how it is handled.

1. Purpose of the App

Patator is designed to communicate with a compatible Bluetooth Low Energy device used in educational, technical, and research projects conducted in partnership with HES-SO Genève.

2. Information processed by the App

Depending on the connected device and the functions used, the App may process:

  • Bluetooth device identifiers and connection status.
  • Sensor measurements transmitted by the connected device, such as temperature and humidity.
  • Date and time associated with measurements.
  • Technical diagnostic information required to establish and maintain the Bluetooth connection.

3. How the information is used

The information is used only to provide the App’s functions, including:

  • Detecting and connecting to the compatible Patator Bluetooth device.
  • Receiving, displaying, and recording sensor measurements.
  • Starting, pausing, and stopping measurement sessions.
  • Allowing the user to review or export data when this function is available.

4. Bluetooth access

The App requires access to Bluetooth in order to discover and communicate with the compatible device. Bluetooth information is used solely for this purpose and is not used for advertising or user tracking.

5. Data storage and transmission

Measurement data may be stored locally on the user’s device for the duration required by the App’s functions. The App does not transmit personal information to advertising networks, data brokers, or third-party analytics services.

If an authorized export or synchronization function is used, data is transmitted only when initiated or enabled by the user and only to the destination configured for the relevant project.

6. Analytics, advertising, and tracking

The App does not include third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs.

7. Data sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties, except where necessary for an explicitly authorized project function or where required by law.

8. Data retention and deletion

Data stored locally by the App may be removed using the App’s available controls, where provided, or by deleting the App from the device. Data exported outside the App must be deleted separately from the destination where it was saved.

9. Children’s privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13 and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Intended use and Terms of Use

Patator is intended exclusively for authorized educational, technical, and research projects conducted in partnership with HES-SO Genève.

Use of the App is subject to the Terms of Use.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the App or its data practices change. The latest version will always be published on this page.
