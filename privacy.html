Privacy Policy

Nos Voyages

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Nos Voyages (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may process, why it is used, and the choices available to you.

1. Information processed by the App

The App may process the following information when you use its features:

  • Precise GPS location.
  • Date and time associated with recorded positions.
  • Travel routes and trip history created by the user.
  • Technical information needed to communicate with the configured travel station or server.
  • Photos associated with a trip, if this feature is used.

2. How the information is used

This information is used only to provide the App’s functions, including:

  • Recording and displaying travel routes.
  • Showing the current position on a map.
  • Synchronizing trip information with a configured station or server.
  • Allowing the user to review or delete recorded travel data.

3. Data storage and transmission

Travel information may be stored on the user’s device and, when synchronization is enabled, transmitted to the server configured for the App. The developer does not sell user data and does not use it for advertising.

Users should ensure that any server they configure is appropriately secured and operated in accordance with applicable privacy requirements.

4. Apple services

The App uses Apple technologies such as MapKit to display maps. Information processed by Apple services is subject to Apple’s own privacy policies and terms.

5. Analytics, advertising, and tracking

The App does not include third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs.

6. Data sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties, except when necessary to operate a service explicitly configured by the user or when required by law.

7. Data retention and deletion

Recorded trips can be deleted using the App’s available controls. Locally stored App data can also be removed by deleting the App from the device. Data stored on a configured server may need to be deleted separately from that server.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13 and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the App or its data practices change. The latest version will always be published on this page.
